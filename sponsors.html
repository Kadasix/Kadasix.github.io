About

Sponsorship funds support DQF's outreach and major events, such as poker tournaments or game nights. In the future, we are considering setting up a quantitative strategies fund.

Benefits

Sponsorship benefits are detailed in the Sponsorship Package, and include coffee chats, resume book access, brand recognition, and exclusive networking opportunities. Since Duke hosts students with excellent exposure to finance and mathematics, sponsors will have first access to a spectacular talent pool.
